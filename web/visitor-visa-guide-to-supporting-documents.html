Part of Apply for a visa to travel through the UK

  1. Step 1 Check if you need a visa

    You might be eligible for different visas depending on why you're visiting the UK and how long for.

    1. Check if you need a visa

  2. Step 2 Prepare the evidence you need

    1. Check what you need to show evidence of
    2. You are currently viewing: Check what documents you can use as evidence

  3. Step 3 Apply for a transit visa

    You should apply for a Visitor in Transit visa if you'll be going through UK border control but leaving the UK within 48 hours

    1. Apply for a Visitor in Transit visa £64

    You should apply for a Direct Airside Transit visa if you won’t go through UK border control (for example, you don't need to check in for your next journey or collect your luggage)

    1. Apply for a Direct Airside Transit visa £35
    1. Check where your visa appointment will be

    You should get a decision within 3 weeks of your appointment.

    You can pay an extra £212 for a decision within 5 working days or £956 for a decision within 1 working day.

  4. or Apply for a visa if you're going through UK border control

    You should apply for a Visitor in Transit visa if you’ll be going through UK border control but leaving the UK within 48 hours.

    1. Apply for a Visitor in Transit visa £64
    2. Check where your visa appointment will be

    You should get a decision within 3 weeks of your appointment.

    You can pay an extra £212 for a decision within 5 working days or £956 for a decision within 1 working day.

  5. Step 4 Attend an appointment

    1. What you need to show at your appointment

    You'll be told when a decision has been made about your visa.

  6. Step 5 Travel to the UK

    1. Check what you need to show at the UK border
    2. Check what you can bring with you

    Keep the documents that prove why you're visiting the UK in your hand luggage so you can show them if asked.

    1. Check the documents you can use to show why you're visiting the UK

    Check your visa to find out when you must leave the UK. If you break the conditions of your visa you may not be able to return to the UK again.
